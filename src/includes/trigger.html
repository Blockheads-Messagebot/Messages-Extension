Triggers

  • The trigger will be checked against chat messages, if it matches, the message will be sent.
  • Triggers are case-insensitive.
  • {{NAME}}, {{Name}}, and {{name}} in the message will be replaced with the user's name.
  • * will be treated as a wildcard.
  • Triggers are matched in order, if more than the max number are matched, later triggers will not be sent.
  • Change the maximum number of responses under settings.
